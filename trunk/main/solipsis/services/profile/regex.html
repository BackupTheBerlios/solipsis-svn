Filters on Solipsis

Filters are insensitive to case, and are matched with any profile or the peer in your surroundings. If you create a filter "sound.wma", it will match both "sound.wma" and "SouND.wma" (and other combinaisons of lower and upper caps). But no other world nor file than "sound.wma". In order to match something like "Sound.wav" or "Sound.mid" or "Best sound ever.mp3", you will have to use wildchars...

The character '*' is available as a wildchar (a joker) to ease and extend filtering results.

For instance the filter "*.mp3" matching anyfile with the extension".mp3". In the first example, you can use the filter "sound.*" to match the "Sound.wav" or "Sound.mid". Another example would be "*madonna*.jpg" that would match any file coutaining "madonna" in its title and with the extension ".mp3"

You may use the wildcar '*' anytime, at the beginning of a world, inside it or at the end. For instance "s*nd" will match "sound", and "silverband" and "s,fefegegeind" and so one.
